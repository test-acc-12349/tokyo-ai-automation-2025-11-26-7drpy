About Tokyo AI Automation

Founded in 2020, Tokyo AI Automation was established with a clear mission: to revolutionize how businesses operate by making intelligent automation accessible to organizations of all sizes. Our founding team, comprising experts in artificial intelligence, enterprise software, and business process optimization, recognized a critical gap in the market. Traditional automation solutions were either too complex, too expensive, or too rigid to adapt to modern business needs. We set out to build a platform that would be powerful yet intuitive, scalable yet affordable, and flexible enough to handle the unique challenges of diverse industries and business models.

At our core, we believe that technology should empower people, not replace them. Our mission is to liberate knowledge workers from repetitive, mundane tasks and enable them to focus on meaningful, strategic work that drives business value. We're committed to continuous innovation, customer success, and building a platform that evolves with the changing needs of modern enterprises. With a team of dedicated professionals and a growing community of satisfied clients across Asia-Pacific, we're transforming industries one automation at a time, proving that intelligent technology and human potential create extraordinary results.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Tokyo AI Automation and how it can benefit your organization.

How long does it take to implement Tokyo AI Automation?

Implementation timelines vary based on your organization's complexity and scope, but typically range from 2-8 weeks. Our streamlined onboarding process includes dedicated support from our implementation team, comprehensive training for your staff, and phased rollout to minimize disruption. Most clients see initial results within the first 2-3 weeks. We provide a detailed project plan during the initial consultation to give you accurate timelines specific to your needs.

Is Tokyo AI Automation secure and compliant with regulations?

Absolutely. Security and compliance are paramount to our platform. We maintain ISO 27001 certification, SOC 2 Type II compliance, and full GDPR adherence. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. We conduct regular security audits, penetration testing, and maintain comprehensive data backup systems. Our platform also supports various industry-specific compliance requirements including HIPAA, PCI-DSS, and financial regulations. Your data remains in secure, redundant data centers with 99.99% uptime guarantee.

Can Tokyo AI Automation integrate with our existing systems?

Yes, our platform is designed for seamless integration with virtually any existing system. We provide pre-built connectors for 500+ popular applications including SAP, Oracle, Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics, and countless others. For custom systems, our robust API and webhook support enable flexible integration. Our technical team works with your IT department to ensure smooth connectivity while maintaining data integrity and security. We also support on-premise, cloud, and hybrid environments, giving you complete flexibility in your infrastructure choices.

What kind of support and training do you provide?

We provide comprehensive support at every stage of your journey. This includes dedicated onboarding specialists, customized training programs for your team, 24/7 technical support via multiple channels, detailed documentation, video tutorials, and regular webinars. Our customer success team proactively monitors your usage and provides optimization recommendations. We also offer advanced training for administrators and power users, quarterly business reviews to ensure you're maximizing ROI, and a vibrant community forum where users share best practices and solutions.

What is the pricing model and how is ROI typically achieved?

Our pricing is flexible and scalable, based on the number of automation processes and monthly transaction volume. We offer transparent, predictable pricing with no hidden fees. Most clients achieve positive ROI within 6-12 months through labor cost savings, error reduction, and improved efficiency. For example, automating invoice processing alone typically saves $50,000-$150,000 annually depending on volume.
